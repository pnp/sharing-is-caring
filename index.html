ABOUT SHARING IS CARING

What is Sharing is Caring?

Sharing is Caring is a PnP program that provides hands-on guidance on using and contributing to the Microsoft365 community.

What is a Sharing is Caring session?

Each Sharing is Caring session provides hands-on, collaborative guidance. Depending on the session topic, this may include how to use Microsoft 365 & PnP resources, how to contribute to the community and more! All sessions provide a unique opportunity to collaborate in a smaller setting.

Are the sessions recorded?

The sessions are not recorded. We want everyone to feel comfortable asking any and all questions in a safe space, without the worry of being recorded.

Can I attend a session more than once?

Absolutely! We understand that there can be alot of information to absorb and attending the sessions more than once can help ensure you grasp the concepts.

Can I ask a question before attending?

Yes!

David Warner , Hugo Bernier or April Dunnam all administer the Sharing is Caring sessions and are happy to answer any questions. Please feel free to send a Twitter direct message.

Community by the community support

PnP is supported in similar ways as other open source projects done by Microsoft with support from the community by the community.

Recommended transparency

There are numerous partners that utilize PnP within their solutions for customers. Support for this is provided by the Partner. When PnP material is used in deployments, we recommend being clear with your customer/deployment owner on the support model.
